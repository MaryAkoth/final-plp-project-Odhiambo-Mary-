Key Benefits for Students
Convenient Booking System: Students can easily schedule appointments online without waiting in queues, making it easier to access healthcare.
Access to Global Expertise: The platform connects students with experienced doctors from the US and India, providing specialized medical advice that may not be locally available.
Improved Health Outcomes: With timely appointments and access to a broader range of expertise, students can receive more accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans.
Educational Support: The system encourages students to take charge of their health, contributing to a healthier school environment.
